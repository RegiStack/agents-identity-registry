# Contributing

Thank you for your interest.

This repository accepts only limited public-facing contributions that preserve
its intentionally narrow scope. Appropriate contributions include:

- editorial clarity
- wording improvements
- conceptual framing refinements
- terminology consistency

Please do not submit:

- implementation proposals
- technical designs
- architectural breakdowns
- process descriptions
- detailed examples
- code, schemas, or protocol material

When in doubt, prefer omission over elaboration.

Maintainers may decline contributions that expand disclosure beyond the
repository's intended public scope.
