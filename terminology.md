# Terminology

## Agent Identity

A conceptual basis for treating an autonomous agent as a governed entity rather
than only as an observed source of activity.

## Authorized Behavior Context

The bounded context within which execution may be regarded as legitimate.

## Execution Permission

The conditional allowance for an agent to act within a governed setting.

## Identity Consistency

The condition in which an acting agent remains sufficiently aligned with its
authorized context to justify execution permission.

## Attestation

A general assertion that relevant conditions have been established or preserved.

These terms are used here at a conceptual level only.
